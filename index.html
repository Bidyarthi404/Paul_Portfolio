About Me

Bidyarthi Paul is an adjunct lecturer and NLP researcher at Southeast University. His research interests include natural language processing, computational linguistics, and multilingual AI systems. He leads the Language Technology research group, which develops innovative NLP solutions for low-resource languages.

His work focuses on creating equitable AI systems that can serve diverse linguistic communities, with a special emphasis on Bengali and other South Asian languages. He believes in the power of technology to bridge communication gaps and foster cross-cultural understanding.
