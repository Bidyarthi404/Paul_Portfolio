About Me

Bidyarthi Paul is a researcher specializing in Natural Language Processing (NLP) with a focus on low-resource languages, particularly Bengali. His research interests include mathematical reasoning in Bengali, regional dialects, and developing AI solutions for under-resourced languages.

He has contributed to several significant research projects and publications in the field of NLP, focusing on Bengali language processing, hate speech detection, and mathematical problem solving.

Currently, he works as an Adjunct Lecturer at Southeast University and as a Computer Science Teacher at Mastermind English Medium School, while continuing his research in NLP.
