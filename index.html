About Me

I am Bidyarthi Paul, a passionate Computer Science researcher dedicated to advancing Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP), with a special focus on low-resource languages, particularly Bengali.

My research covers Bengali mathematical reasoning, regional dialect analysis, hate speech detection, and the development of large language models. I have contributed to numerous datasets and publications, aiming to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical real-world solutions. Beyond research, I actively teach and mentor, inspiring the next generation of innovators in AI and data science.
